Pizza Recipe

Pizza is a dish originated from Italy, it is made with addign cheese and sauce on top of a dough and then baking it in a oven. Pizza is authentically baked in a woodfire oven.

ingredients:

      Dough
      Pizza sauce
      cheese
      topping of your choice

Steps:

  1. Prepare a pizza dough
  2. prepare pizza sauce and cheese
  3. add pizza sauce on the dough
  4. add cheese on top
  5. bake it in an oven till cooked
